    Open Architecture

    An Open Architecture

    Where wireless and web come together as one

      Physical spaces...

    ...are occupied by tens of billions of radio-identifiable devices, capable of interfacing with radio infrastructure through wireless standards.

      Digital applications...

    ...are enabled by the integration of countless software packages, technology stacks and platforms through web standards.

    Pareto Anywhere is at the centre of an open technology architecture, connecting the individual elements of the above in any combination, from any mix of vendors.

    Tens of billions of radio-identifiable devices are shipping annually. Countless vendors, including reelyActive, offer radio-identification and location infrastructure. Our open source middleware translates their output into a real-time, web-standard data stream to feed any database and/or drive any web application.

    Radio-identifiable devices

    Representing people, products and places at a human scale

    Are Bluetooth devices compatible?
    Yes. Specifically Bluetooth Low Energy devices that transmit advertising packets. These include beacons as well as most smartphones, wearables and smart appliances.
      As of 2018, there are approximiately 4 billion Bluetooth devices shipping annually!
    Are RFID devices compatible?
    Yes. Pareto Anywhere's core data structure is capable of representing any RFID device. Support for RAIN RFID is prioritised as the current leading standard.
      As of 2018, there are approximately 15 billion RAIN RFID devices shipping annually!

    Radio-identification infrastructure

    Detecting & locating people, products and places at a human scale

    Is there compatible reelyActive infrastructure?
    Yes. Pareto Anywhere works seamlessly with reelyActive's own plug-and-play hardware infrastructure.
      Owl-in-One (BLE)   Reelceiver (BLE)   Reelceiver (915MHz active RFID)
    Is there compatible third-party infrastructure?
    Yes. Any receiver/transceiver providing access to the packet-level data stream is compatible with Pareto Anywhere software.

    Databases and analytics suites

    Storing the real-time data stream and facilitating analysis over time

    Is there a preferred stack?
    Yes. Pareto Anywhere integrates seamlessly with the open source Elastic Stack, using Elasticsearch as a database, Kibana as an analytics tool and APM as a monitoring tool.
