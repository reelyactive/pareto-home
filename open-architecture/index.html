    Open Architecture

    An Open Architecture

    With Pareto Anywhere at its centre

    Pareto Anywhere is at the centre of an open technology architecture consisting of:

    • radio-identifiable devices
    • radio-identification and location infrastructure
    • analytics and application software

    Pareto Anywhere leverages existing standards to connect the above in any combination, from any mix of vendors.

    What for?

    Any application. Any physical space.

    Standards foster interoperability. Interoperability creates opportunity.

    Pareto Anywhere pioneers a vendor-agnostic approach to harness and channel the novel data stream afforded by a critical mass of standard radio-identifiable devices and infrastructure.

    Extend the power of the web to physical applications

    Pareto Anywhere is open source. Explore all use cases.
