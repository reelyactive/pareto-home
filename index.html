    Platform

    A standard, digital representation of who/what is where/how drives real-time business rules and analytics for data-based decision making.

    Pareto Anywhere integrates seamlessly with the Elastic Stack for search, analytics and beautiful dashboards*.

    A suite of web apps facilitate discovery and development, with simple yet powerful APIs for integration with third-party software and applications.

    * we won an Elastic Search Award for that!

    Pareto Anywhere is continuously evolving, community-driven, permissively licensed open source software.

    Run anywhere: edge, local and/or cloud.

    Learn more

    Pareto Anywhere is infrastructure-agnostic and can ingest data from, and often even run on, third-party gateways and existing in-building infrastructure.

    Owl-in-One gateways and novel daisy-chain reelceiver infrastructure by reelyActive are, of course, also compatible.

    Infrastructure

    Pareto Anywhere is radio-protocol-agnostic and can ingest ambient wireless packets of any origin via compatible infrastructure.

    Billions of Bluetooth Low Energy devices provide an ample source of ambient data, while beacons can be introduced to satisfy specific use cases. Long-range RFID represents an even greater untapped potential.
