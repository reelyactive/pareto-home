    Questions?

      Yes   Contact tracing is just one of several possible applications of interaction detection. Pareto Anywhere supports both real-time and digest-based contact tracing.

      Bluetooth Low Energy   The ability for devices to both transmit and receive messages to all peers in range makes Bluetooth Low Energy an ideal technology for interaction detection. Pareto Anywhere is nonetheless radio-protocol-agnostic.

      Third-Party   Many third-party vendors offer Bluetooth badges and bracelets capable of supporting DirAct embedded software. Devices with standard or documented payloads are also supported by Pareto Anywhere.

      DirAct   Co-developed by reelyActive, DirAct is embedded software that can run on common third-party devices, enabling these to detect and relay the presence of their peers.
