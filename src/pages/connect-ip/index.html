Connect your starter kit in two easy steps

The hub will relay the sensor data to the Pareto cloud platform.

Not your starter kit? Click here.

1. Apply Power

Plug the included power adapter in to a mains outlet. Plug the adapter cable in to the barrel connector on the hub.

2. Connect to Network

Connect the LAN2 port of the hub to a free port on your network switch/router using a standard network cable (not included).

Your hub is now relaying the sensor data to the Pareto cloud platform. Log in and begin your trial!
