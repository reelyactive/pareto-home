What does it do?

The included reelceiver detects compatible Bluetooth Low Energy devices in range and streams their wireless packets to a computer via the included USB hub.

In other words, the kit detects and identifies wireless devices such as smartphones, wearables and electronic appliances that are present in your space.

How do I use the data?

Our Pareto platform provides a visualisation of what's detected in your space, a real-time dashboard showing traffic patterns, and the ability to relay the data stream to any digital platform in a single click.

Our team will ensure you get comfortably up and running once you receive your kit.

What do I need?

All you need is a computer (Windows, Mac, Linux) with a free USB port and an Internet connection.

What's included?

  • One Bluetooth Low Energy reelceiver
  • One reel-to-USB hub
  • Three-month trial of Pareto
