Questions? Shoot.

Must I start with a kit?

Yes, you must. The trial is designed to ensure that your expectations will be met, providing ample time to select the right plan.

What happens when I receive my kit?

You'll follow the instructions on our connect page and the 3-month trial will begin.

Do I get to keep the starter kit?

Yes, you do. The sensor and hub are yours to keep.

What if I need more time?

Simply extend your trial with the Base Pareto plan for as long as you need. We'll be in touch before the 3 months expire.

Are there volume discounts?

Yes, and our sales team will be pleased to discuss these over the course of your trial.

Why pay if the open source is free?

The Pareto service means we take care of all hosting and maintenance for you, at any scale.
