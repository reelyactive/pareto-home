Questions? Shoot.

Must I start with a kit?

Yes, you must. The trial establishes a relationship where we can best ensure that your expectations will be met.

What happens when I receive my kit?

You'll follow the instructions on our connect page and the 3-month trial will begin.

Do I get to keep the starter kit?

Yes, you do. The sensor is yours to keep.

What sensors are supported?

Pareto currently supports all reelyActive sensors, and we're actively working to support third-party sensors.

Are the sensors sold separately?

Yes, the Pareto SaaS and the sensor hardware are sold independently — the starter kit being the sole exception.

Why pay if the open source is free?

Enjoy industry-leading features and let us take care of hosting and maintenance for you — at any scale!
