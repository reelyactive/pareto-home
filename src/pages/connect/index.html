Connect your starter kit in two easy steps

The Owl-in-One will relay the sensor data to the Pareto cloud platform.

Not your starter kit? Click here.

1. Connect Ethernet

Connect the Owl-in-One to a free Ethernet port on your router/switch.

2. Apply power

Connect the Owl-in-One to AC power via the provided adapter. Alternatively, it can be powered by a USB port that provides 1A of power.

The Owl-in-One is now relaying the sensor data to the Pareto cloud platform. Log in and begin your trial!
